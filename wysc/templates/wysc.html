Pairing Format

Day 1 - Swiss draw with no lags and no repeats

Day 2 - Swiss draw with no lags 1 repeat
(At the end of the second day, a player may have met the same opponent a maximum of two times)

Day 3 - Swiss draw with no lags and 2 repeats until round 23
(Which means at the end of round 23, a player may have faced the same opponent upto 3 times). The 24th round introduces one more repeat.
