About Us

World Youth Scrabble Championship

The World Youth Scrabble Championship (formerly known as the WESPA Youth Cup) has been contested every year since 2006. This year, Sri Lanka has been offered the opportunity to host the tournament
youthscrabble.org


Participating Countries


Sri Lanka International Open

The Sri Lanka International Open Scrabble Championship is a renowned and highly competitive event, attracting top-rated players from all over the globe. It will return this year for its 8th edition.
scrabble.lk


Scrabble Federation of Sri Lanka

The Scrabble Federation of Sri Lanka (SFSL), the governing body for Scrabble in the island will be the main organizer for WYSC 2024 and SLIO 2024
scrabble.lk

WESPA

The World English-Language Scrabble Players' Association (WESPA) is the overarching global body for English-language national Scrabble associations and similar entities.

Tournament Venue

Citrus Waskaduwa, a five star beach hotel, will host the prestigious WYSC & SLIO . It’s 1.5 hours drive from the Colombo International airport and about 1.5 hours drive from the Colombo City business area during rush hour. The Galle Fort, a famous UNESCO world heritage site, is 1.5 hours drive in the opposite direction.

Transportation

Airport Pickups

Airport Pickups can be arranged by the organisers at a fee for players arriving at Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB).

Taxi Hailing

Uber and Pick Me taxi services can easily be accessed around the Colombo, Kaluthara Area

Accomodation

Citrus Waskaduwa

Accommodations are available at Citrus Waskaduwa at revised rates for all players taking part in the WYSC & SLIO
